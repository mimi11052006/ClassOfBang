About Clash of BaNG

Clash of BaNG is a growing fan community website inspired by the world-famous Clash-style fantasy universe. This official website is launched to represent our identity, improve member experience, and centralize all guides, troop information, and clan activity.

📜

Our History

Originally started as a small online forum in 2018. The community provides various content including troop guides, hero strategies, fan art collections, and beginner tutorials for players.

🏆

Key Milestones

Thanks to our high-quality content and active player base, we are growing rapidly. We have successfully expanded into three regional community hubs located in Jakarta, Bandung, and Sanur.

👥

The Founders

Created by passionate gamers whose most beloved units are the Dark Troops. The founders want to expand their reach to attract more fans worldwide through this new platform.
