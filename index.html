About Clash of BaNG

Originally started as a small online forum in 2018, Clash of BaNG has quickly expanded into three regional community hubs located in Jakarta, Bandung, and Sanur.

The community provides various content including troop guides, hero strategies, fan art collections, and beginner tutorials, with a special focus on Dark Troops—the most beloved by our founders.

Thanks to high-quality content and an active player base, Clash of BaNG is growing rapidly. We're expanding our reach to attract more fans worldwide and provide the ultimate gaming experience.
